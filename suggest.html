Places For Visit In India.

Shimla

temperature : 6°C

Best Time To Visit:-

Summer (March to June): This is considered the peak tourist season in Shimla. The weather during these months is pleasant with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 30°C (59°F to 86°F).

Autumn (October to November):In Autumn visit Shimla when the weather remains pleasant with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 25°C (50°F to 77°F).

Manali

temperature : 6°C

Best Time To Visit:-

Summer (March to June): Like Shimla, summer is the peak tourist season in Manali. During these months, the weather is pleasant with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 25°C (50°F to 77°F).

Autumn (October to November): Autumn is another favorable time to visit Manali when the weather remains pleasant and the monsoon recedes. Temperatures range from 5°C to 20°C (41°F to 68°F).

Kashmir

temperature : 6°C

Best Time To Visit:-

Spring (March to May): Spring is a lovely time to visit Kashmir when the valley is in full bloom with colorful flowers, particularly the famous tulips in April. The weather during this season is mild and pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 20°C (50°F to 68°F).

Summer (June to August): Summer in Kashmir is relatively warm with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 30°C (59°F to 86°F).

Autumn (September to November): The weather remains pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 25°C (50°F to 77°F).

Kedarnath

temperature : 6°C

Best Time To Visit:-

Summer (May to June): Summer is the primary tourist season in Kedarnath. During this time, the weather is generally mild and pleasant with temperatures ranging from 5°C to 20°C (41°F to 68°F).

Autumn (October to November): Autumn is another good time to visit Kedarnath when the weather is cool and pleasant. The temperatures range from 0°C to 15°C (32°F to 59°F).

Kerala

temperature : 6°C

Best Time To Visit:-

Winter (October to February): Winter is considered the best time to visit Kerala as the weather is pleasant and relatively cooler. Temperatures range from 20°C to 32°C (68°F to 90°F).

Summer (March to May): Kerala experiences hot and humid weather during the summer months. Temperatures can soar above 35°C (95°F) in some regions.

Goa

temperature : 6°C

Best Time To Visit:-

Winter (November to February): Winter is considered the best time to visit Goa as the weather is pleasant and relatively cooler. Temperatures range from 20°C to 30°C (68°F to 86°F).

Summer (March to May): Goa experiences hot and humid weather during the summer months. Temperatures can soar above 35°C (95°F).
